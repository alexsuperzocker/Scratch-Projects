Dungeon Shoota - by Alex and Paul

Player 1 (Red) Controlls:
W,A,S,D - move around
R - reload
Spacebar - shoot

Player 2 (Blue) Controlls:
Arrow keys - move around
1 - reload
0 - shoot

Shop:
To buy a weapon, click it, click "buy" and then click it again to be able to select it as your next weapon.

The weapon you selected previously will not be the one you have next round (-> reselect the weapon)
